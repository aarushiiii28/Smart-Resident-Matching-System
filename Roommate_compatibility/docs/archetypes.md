# Lifestyle Archetypes

## Disciplined

Characteristics:

* Sleeps early
* Wakes early
* High academic seriousness
* High cleanliness
* High punctuality
* Low gaming
* Low guest frequency
* Low late-night calls
* High schedule consistency
* High expense responsibility

Typical Values:

* sleep_time: 21-23
* wake_time: 5-7
* cleanliness_score: 4-5
* academic_seriousness: 4-5

---

## Average

Characteristics:

* Balanced routine
* Moderate study habits
* Moderate cleanliness
* Average social activity
* Average privacy needs

Typical Values:

* Most features between 2-4
