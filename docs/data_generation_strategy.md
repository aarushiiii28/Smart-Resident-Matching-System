# Data Generation Strategy

## 1. Lifestyle Features

These are the parent features that influence many other behaviors.

* lifestyle_type
* schedule_consistency
* adaptability
* social_energy
* academic_seriousness

---

## 2. Sleep Features

These features determine sleeping and waking behavior.

* sleep_time
* wake_time
* alarm_count
* snooze_frequency
* noise_sleep_tolerance
* lights_off_time

---

## 3. Study Features

These describe academic and study-related habits.

* study_hours
* study_time_preference
* silence_requirement
* self_talking_study
* academic_seriousness

---

## 4. Cleanliness Features

These describe hygiene and room maintenance habits.

* cleanliness_score
* organization_score
* laundry_frequency
* bed_making_habit
* room_cleaning_frequency
* bathing_frequency
* towel_management

---

## 5. Social Features

These describe interaction with roommates and visitors.

* phone_call_frequency
* late_night_calls
* talkativeness
* guest_frequency
* overnight_guest_comfort
* friendship_expectation
* guest_timing_preference

---

## 6. Privacy Features

These describe boundary and sharing preferences.

* privacy_preference
* sharing_comfort
* boundary_importance
* unauthorized_usage
* borrowing_frequency
* independence_preference

---

## 7. Environment Features

These describe room environment preferences.

* fan_speed_preference
* temperature_preference
* entry_exit_noise_awareness

---

## 8. Personality Features

These describe emotional and behavioral tendencies.

* conflict_tolerance
* conflict_style
* criticism_response
* emotional_stability
* negativity_level
* communication_respect
* punctuality
* adaptability

---

## 9. Lifestyle Habits

These describe day-to-day living preferences.

* smoking
* drinking
* gaming_hours
* music_frequency
* music_volume
* room_eating_habit
* food_smell_tolerance
* food_preference
* gym_frequency
* religious_practice_frequency

---

## 10. Financial Features

These describe responsibility in shared expenses.

* expense_responsibility

---

# Feature Relationships

## Disciplined Student

Likely Characteristics:

* Early sleep_time
* Early wake_time
* High cleanliness_score
* High organization_score
* High academic_seriousness
* Low guest_frequency
* Low gaming_hours
* High punctuality

---

## Night Owl Student

Likely Characteristics:

* Late sleep_time
* Late wake_time
* More late_night_calls
* More gaming_hours
* Lower schedule_consistency

---

## Social Student

Likely Characteristics:

* High guest_frequency
* High talkativeness
* High friendship_expectation
* High social_energy
* Lower privacy_preference

---

## Introvert Student

Likely Characteristics:

* High privacy_preference
* High independence_preference
* Low guest_frequency
* Low talkativeness
* Low friendship_expectation

---

## Clean Student

Likely Characteristics:

* High cleanliness_score
* High organization_score
* Frequent room_cleaning_frequency
* Good towel_management

---

## Careless Student

Likely Characteristics:

* Low cleanliness_score
* Low organization_score
* Poor towel_management
* Infrequent room_cleaning_frequency
